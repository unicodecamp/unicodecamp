Taking our InOut a step further and distinguishing it from a private hack-competition, we’re coming together with “InOut in your city” in our fourth edition. “InOut in your city” is first of it’s kind in India and will certainly be helpful in driving it’s innovative agenda of making a nationwide developer community with a hackathon post conference. This initiative is for many potential tech groups in prominent colleges of India, who aspire to run a hackathon in their colleges. InOut will help you organise one of your own and direct in keeping IT professionals and new startups across the country connected and educated.

If you are a passionate individual with ambitious dreams and would like to work with talented group of tech loving people, then the opportunity is right for you. It is a platform where you can make a difference by contributing, but at the same time enhance your marketing skills & expertise.
