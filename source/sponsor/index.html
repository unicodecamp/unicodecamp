Hackathons are communities based on learning, teaching, and creating. Thus, our focus is to build a platform to facilitate collaboration between sponsors and hackers, and enable great minds to join forces to create cool hacks. In addition, a hallmark of InOut is that we accept only the brightest of all applicants (compared to last year’s 4000+ total applicants), who're selected on the basis of their experience, open source contributions, demonstrated passion and aptitude for technology & innovation.
